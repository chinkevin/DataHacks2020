\subsection{Permutation Test}\label{permutation-test}}

Test if the distribution of obesity rates for West Virginia is by chance
or not.

Our test statistic will be the observed obesity rate in West Virginia:
\textasciitilde{}35\%.

\emph{Null Hypothesis:} the population of West Virginia's obesity
prevalence come from the same distribution as the population of the US's
obesity prevalence. \emph{Alternative Hypothesis:} the populations come
from different distributions.

Set a significance level of 0.01

We will assume that the survey gathered data from 1000 individuals to
approximate the obesity rate, so we will use a sample size of 1000.
